About The Chamber

        

          The Abu Dhabi Chamber provides all the means necessary to support its
          members, investors, entrepreneurs and businessmen, including
          facilitating access to trade requirements and smart services.
        

        

          The Chamber undertakes six main tasks that go beyond providing
          services to other dimensions that contribute to sponsoring and
          supporting national economic development. The Chamber is committed to
          following Abu Dhabi’s approach in adopting the principles of
          scientific and modern strategic planning to complete the development
          process of the economic and investment sectors in the Emirate of Abu
          Dhabi and the UAE over the next five decades, and to maximize its
          contribution to achieving Abu Dhabi’s economic goals and both the “We
          are the UAE 2031” vision and “The UAE Vision 2071”, which contribute
          to highlighting the country’s successful economic model, and to pave
          the way towards a more accomplished and brighter future.
        

        

          The Chamber adopts a comprehensive and integrated development strategy
          for the years (2023 - 2025) that simulates the radical transformations
          that the world is witnessing today, and keeps pace with Abu Dhabi’s
          priorities in building a diversified and sustainable knowledge
          economy. It also includes innovative and ambitious plans that
          establish a qualitative leap in the Chamber’s performance, its work
          system and services, and its support for the business community. It
          ensures its effective contribution to advancing sustainable
          development witnessed by the capital, supporting the private sector,
          enhancing its competitive capabilities and enabling it to keep pace
          with global economic changes, by facilitating trade exchange
          opportunities, supporting policies, and adopting innovation and
          digital transformations.
