            About Us
          

          

            The Abu Dhabi Chamber provides all the means necessary to support
            its members, investors, entrepreneurs and businessmen, including
            facilitating access to trade requirements and smart services.
          

          

            The Abu Dhabi Chamber provides all the means necessary to support
            its members, investors, entrepreneurs and businessmen, including
            facilitating access to trade requirements and smart services. The
            Chamber undertakes six main tasks that go beyond providing services
            to other dimensions that contribute to sponsoring and supporting
            national economic development.
