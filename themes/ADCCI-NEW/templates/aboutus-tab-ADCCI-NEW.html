Our History

                    

                      Serving business community since 1969 & till today
                    

                    

                      The Abu Dhabi Chamber of Commerce and Industry was
                      established in 1969 pursuant to a royal decree issued by
                      the late Sheikh Zayed bin Sultan Al Nahyan in his capacity
                      as the Ruler of Abu Dhabi with the emergence of the need
                      for a unified body working to support private companies
                      operating in the emirate and assist them in promoting
                      their business and protecting their interests, to convey
                      the voice of the private sector and to embody its status
                      as a pivotal platform to support the growth of the
                      business sector, care for its interests, and enable it to
                      reach its fullest potential by identifying its current and
                      future needs and communicating them to regulatory bodies,
                      opening horizons of communication and cooperation to
                      support the growth of its business, and providing
                      comprehensive digital services that meet its aspirations.
                    

                  

                  

                    

                      

                        
                      

                      

                        
                      

                    

                  

                


                
              

              

                
                

                  

                    
About The Chamber

                    

                      The Abu Dhabi Chamber provides all the means necessary to
                      support its members, investors, entrepreneurs and
                      businessmen, including facilitating access to trade
                      requirements and smart services.
                    

                    

                      The Chamber undertakes six main tasks that go beyond
                      providing services to other dimensions that contribute to
                      sponsoring and supporting national economic development.
                      The Chamber is committed to following Abu Dhabi’s approach
                      in adopting the principles of scientific and modern
                      strategic planning to complete the development process of
                      the economic and investment sectors in the Emirate of Abu
                      Dhabi and the UAE over the next five decades, and to
                      maximize its contribution to achieving Abu Dhabi’s
                      economic goals and both the “We are the UAE 2031” vision
                      and “The UAE Vision 2071”, which contribute to
                      highlighting the country’s successful economic model, and
                      to pave the way towards a more accomplished and brighter
                      future.
                    

                    

                      The Chamber adopts a comprehensive and integrated
                      development strategy for the years (2023 - 2025) that
                      simulates the radical transformations that the world is
                      witnessing today, and keeps pace with Abu Dhabi’s
                      priorities in building a diversified and sustainable
                      knowledge economy. It also includes innovative and
                      ambitious plans that establish a qualitative leap in the
                      Chamber’s performance, its work system and services, and
                      its support for the business community. It ensures its
                      effective contribution to advancing sustainable
                      development witnessed by the capital, supporting the
                      private sector, enhancing its competitive capabilities and
                      enabling it to keep pace with global economic changes, by
                      facilitating trade exchange opportunities, supporting
                      policies, and adopting innovation and digital
                      transformations.

CEO Message

                          

                            Members and Partners of the Abu Dhabi Chamber,
                            Greetings,
                          

                          

                            Under the wise guidance of His Highness Sheikh
                            Mohamed bin Zayed Al Nahyan. The UAE's prominent
                            standing as a key player in the global economy
                            participant is already well established and our aim
                            now is to propel this legacy even further.
                          

                          

                            
                          

                          

                            The Abu Dhabi Chamber stands as a beacon for the
                            business community, focusing on strengthening
                            relationships and partnerships, while fostering an
                            environment conducive for growth and investment in
                            Abu Dhabi. Our mission is to provide unwavering
                            support, connections, and dedicated advocacy for the
                            Abu Dhabi business community. Our goal is to
                            solidify Abu Dhabi’s position as a premier
                            destination for businesses and talent in the MENA
                            region by 2025, in alignment with the UAE's
                            ambitious Fifty Economic Plan.As we move forward, we
                            are committed to maintaining the highest standards
                            of transparency, communication, and trust at the Abu
                            Dhabi Chamber. We strive to ensure that the Chamber
                            stands as an organization that values its members,
                            encourages innovation, and fosters growth.The
                            strength of the Chamber resides in its strategic
                            partners, whose capabilities we trust to play a
                            pivotal role in achieving our strategic objectives
                            and elevating the Chamber's standing. Together, we
                            can propel advancement across all economic sectors,
                            building on the successes of the last decade to
                            shape the next.
                          

                          
Ahmed Khalifa Al Qubaisi

                          
CEO
