              We strive to empower businesses in Abu Dhabi enabling the private
              sector to reach its full potential within a competitive
              state-of-the-art ecosystem.
            

            

              The Abu Dhabi Chamber provides all the means necessary to support
              its members, investors, entrepreneurs and businessmen, including
              facilitating access to trade requirements and smart services. The
              Chamber undertakes six main tasks that go beyond providing
              services to other dimensions that contribute to sponsoring and
              supporting national economic development.
