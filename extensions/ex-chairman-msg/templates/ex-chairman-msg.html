A Message from the Chairman

H.E. Eng. Awaidha Murshed Al Marar
Chairman

H.E. Awaidha Al Marar and a number of leaders from different fields are leading the DoE towards a sustainable future for the coming generations.

H.E. Al Marar is a member of the Abu Dhabi Executive Council and serves as the Chairman of the Department of Energy and is a Board Member in the Supreme Petroleum Council in Abu Dhabi, Abu Dhabi Development Holding Company, Emirates Nuclear Energy Corporation, Nawah Energy Company, and Barakah One Company. In addition, H.E. is a member of several committees in the Emirate of Abu Dhabi, such as the Executive Committee of the Emirates Nuclear Energy Corporation and the Nawah Energy Company. H.E. Al Marar holds a Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering from the University of Arizona (USA) and a Master of Business Administration (MBA) from UAE University’s College of Business and Economics. The development of the UAE energy sector is pivotal in establishing our country as a regional and international leader in the diversification and adoption of clean and renewable energy, and for ensuring that our citizens continue to enjoy a high standard of living. Our insightful and visionary leaders and governing bodies have been fundamental in creating innovative and inspirational initiatives that will enable us to achieve sustainability within the energy sector. The Abu Dhabi Department of Energy is proud to play a leading role in establishing Abu Dhabi as a pioneer in the use of clean and renewable energy and in driving even greater levels of energy efficiency across the industry.
