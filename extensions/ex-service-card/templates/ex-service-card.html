Request for a Scholarship

Cycle time Six months Approx. cost AED 0

Description

Through this service, high achieving twelfth grade and undergraduate students can apply for scholarships in areas that meet the market requirements.

Conditions

A copy of the valid passport for the student (including the unified number page)

A copy of the enrollment summary (student page and paterfamilias page)
A copy of valid ID card (both sides)
Passport photo with white background
A copy of the final certificate for the ninth, tenth, eleventh and twelfth grades
A copy of the certificate of academic admission in one of the specializations / universities recommended by the ADEK
A copy of the letter stating the status of the national service (for male students only)
A copy of a valid result certificate of EmSAT-English, IELTS or TOEFL IBT exam
A copy of a valid result certificate of EmSAT-Math 

Process

1
Submit the application form and attach the required documents through the website.

2
Attendance require for an interview after completion all requirements.

3
Receive a notification with acceptance or rejection.
